Bangladesh's first open tech cooperative

EquiSaaS BD connects fair learning, accountable contribution, and Bangladesh-first product direction inside one cooperative system.

This model exists to replace exploitative unpaid work with a clearer path: learn in one department, submit proof, get reviewed, and contribute to something real.

The mission begins with a grassroots builder network across all 64 districts of Bangladesh and grows toward practical B2B SaaS for local SMEs.

How the cooperative works

One ecosystem for learning, proof, review, and product direction

Members join one department at a time and move through guided lessons, proof-based tasks, review workflows, and approval-backed records.

The LMS is not just a course catalog. It is the operating layer for learning, proof submission, review, points integrity, and internal coordination.

That structure helps the cooperative grow with evidence and responsibility instead of noise and guesswork.
