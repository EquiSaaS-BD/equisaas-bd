Orientation summary

One video that explains the full mission

This orientation introduces EquiSaaS BD as Bangladesh's first open tech cooperative, where members from all 64 districts can learn, contribute to structured workflows, and move toward shared ownership through accountable contribution.

Founder and CEO Kholipha Ahmmad Al-Amin frames this mission around fair opportunity, practical skill building, and a sweat equity model that lets contributors build and own a real company together.
