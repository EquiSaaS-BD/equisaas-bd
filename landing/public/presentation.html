The Sweat Equity Model

How our cooperative retains ownership and distributes profits

Point System

Members earn equity points based on the tasks and tickets they successfully complete. The higher the contribution, the higher their ownership percentage.

70% Profit Distribution

When a product is sold, 70% of profits are distributed directly to squad members. Revolutionary Publishing: Independent creators from any district can publish their hard-built products through us and keep 70% of the profits.

30% Platform Fund

The remaining 30% is strictly reserved for server costs, marketing, and the corporate platform fund to fuel future expansion.
